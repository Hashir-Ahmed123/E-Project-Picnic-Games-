About This Resort

Mirror Lake Lodge is nestled in a pristine mountain valley, surrounded by towering peaks and crystal-clear waters. Our eco-friendly lodge offers the perfect blend of rustic charm and modern comfort, with stunning lake views from every room.

The resort features private docks, canoe rentals, hiking trails, and picnic areas along the shoreline. Each cabin is crafted from sustainable materials and features local artwork, cozy fireplaces, and private decks overlooking the water.
