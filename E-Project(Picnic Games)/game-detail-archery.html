About This Game

Experience the excitement of combat archery in this safe, action-packed team sport. Combining the thrill of archery with the strategy of dodgeball, players use foam-tipped arrows to eliminate opponents and capture objectives.

Our purpose-built archery tag arenas feature obstacles, bunkers, and targets for dynamic gameplay. Professional referees ensure safety and fair play while providing tactical guidance for teams of all skill levels.
