About This Game

Experience the unique sensation of walking on water with our Aqua Zorb adventure. Step inside our giant inflatable spheres and roll, bounce, and float across the water in this fun and safe activity.

Perfect for families, friends, and team-building events, Aqua Zorb combines laughter, excitement, and a bit of friendly competition. Our zorbs are designed for maximum safety and fun, with plenty of ventilation and secure harness systems.
