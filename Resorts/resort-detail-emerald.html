About This Resort

The Emerald Lawn is a magnificent countryside estate spanning 25 acres of perfectly manicured gardens, rolling lawns, and elegant outdoor spaces. Our resort combines the charm of traditional countryside living with modern luxury amenities.

Perfect for families, corporate events, and special occasions, the resort features multiple lawn areas for games, a Victorian-style tea house, and beautifully appointed rooms overlooking the gardens. Each space is designed for both relaxation and active outdoor enjoyment.
